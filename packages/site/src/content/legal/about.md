# About Deepink

Deepink is a privacy-focused note-taking app built for people who think in details and move between contexts all day. It stays out of the way when a note is simple, and it holds up when the work gets complex.

Deepink exists for one reason: notes should be a **reliable tool**, not a second job.

## What Deepink stands for

### Privacy that holds up under scrutiny
Privacy is not a setting and not a promise to “trust us.” Deepink is designed so your vault remains private by default, and the security model is meant to be understandable, verifiable, and practical.

### Clarity over complexity
Deepink favors features that reduce mental load: fewer places to check, fewer duplicates to maintain, fewer moments of “where did I put that?”

### Ergonomics, not busywork
Good tools respect attention. Deepink is shaped around common tasks and refined to remove unnecessary steps, so the flow stays smooth when the day is full.

### One system, clear boundaries
Work, personal life, learning, long-term projects—these can live together without blending into each other. Deepink is built around clear separation inside a single vault, so context switching stays simple.

## How Deepink earns trust

- **Transparent engineering:** Deepink is open source, with code that can be reviewed.
- **Security-minded by design:** encryption covers the vault structure, not just the obvious parts.
- **Practical decisions:** features are built to be used daily, not to look impressive in a checklist.

## Who Deepink is for

Deepink fits people who take notes because they **work**, **build**, **study**, **decide**, and **remember**—and who want their notes to stay organized and private without turning note-taking into a hobby.
